About Phoenix

Reborn from the ordinary into the extraordinary — a community for donating and trading pre-loved items.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Phoenix started as a small group of friends who believed that every item deserves a second life. Today, we're a global community of millions.

Our Mission

To reduce waste and build a circular economy by making it easy and safe to donate, trade, and sell items you no longer need.

Our Values

Trust, sustainability, community, and transparency. Every interaction is backed by verified profiles and secure messaging.

Why Phoenix?

We believe that the things we own can have many lives. By passing them on, we reduce waste, save resources, and connect with others. Phoenix is where items rise again — from your hands to a new story.
